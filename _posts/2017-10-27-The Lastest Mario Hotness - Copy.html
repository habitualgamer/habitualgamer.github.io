The lastest, greatest Mario game is finally out on the Nintendo Switch. Not bad timing, too!

We're just about more than half a year from the Switch's intial lauch, and it's flagship hero Mario is ready for another 3D platform adventure. Call it an "odyssey" of sorts. The story doens't stray away from it's previous games. Princess Peach is kidnapped by Bowser again, and it's up to Mario and its ghosly assistant to track Bowser through numerous of worlds called 'kingdoms'. New powers include the ablity to possess other enemies that provide some depth to the typcial platform gameplay.

The game is full retail price, but I'm sure it's more than worth it. Once I get a Switch, this will be one of the games on my list!
